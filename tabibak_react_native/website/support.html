📚 Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

1. Open the Tabibok Health app
2. Browse doctors by specialty or search for a specific doctor
3. View available time slots
4. Select a time and confirm your booking
5. You'll receive a confirmation notification

How do I cancel or reschedule an appointment?

Go to "My Appointments" in the app, select the appointment you want to change, and choose "Cancel" or "Reschedule". Please provide reasonable notice to respect the doctor's time.

Is my medical information secure?

Yes. All your data is encrypted and stored securely using industry-standard security measures. We comply with healthcare data protection regulations. Only authorized healthcare providers involved in your care can access your medical information.

Can I access my medical records?

Yes. You can view your complete medical history, prescriptions, visit notes, and uploaded documents anytime through the app's "Medical Records" section.

Will I receive appointment reminders?

Yes. You'll receive push notifications to remind you of upcoming appointments. Make sure notifications are enabled in your device settings.

How do I create an account?

Download the Tabibok Health app from the App Store or Google Play Store. Enter your phone number, verify it with the OTP code sent to you, and complete your profile.

Is Tabibok Health a medical device?

No. Tabibok Health is NOT a medical device. It does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any medical condition. It's a booking and communication platform that connects you with healthcare providers. Always consult qualified healthcare professionals for medical advice.

What should I do in a medical emergency?

Call emergency services immediately. Tabibok Health is for scheduling appointments only and should not be used for emergencies.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. Please note that some information may be retained as required by healthcare regulations.

Can I book appointments for family members?

Yes. You can manage appointments for family members through your account. Add them in the "Family Members" section of your profile.
